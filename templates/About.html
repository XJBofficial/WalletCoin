About WalletCoin

A new cryptocurrency is here!

WalletCoin is a new cryptocurrency which developed and released in 2022
Its a very easy to use cryptocurrency on the www with a very easy REST API
for developers. The mission for WalletCoin is to solve economical problems.
and give to game developers an easy way to monetize their game ( scroll
down for more information ). At the begining, Buy a big amount of WalletCoins
and wait until its price increased!

The new investment method for Gamers !!

-Hey... What do you mean " for Gamers "... maybe you mean " for investors "
-No, I mean for Gamers !!! Yeah its very cringe but... the idea of WalletCoin
is not to be a simple cryptocurrency... WalletCoin is a way to make money by
investing in games. In few words, it is a Stock Market for games !!! You can
create a page for your game in WalletCoin's web and define an amount of
stocks, Other investors will buy these stocks using their WalletCoins. The value
of your game will be increased when someone pay to play your game or donate
it ( using its REST API ). The value of stocks is defined with this way:
Divide the game's economy ( the amount of WalletCoins it has got ) with the
amount of stocks its users owns and you will find the value of one Stock
( Your Game's economy ) / ( The amount of stocks owned by users )

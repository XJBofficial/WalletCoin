WalletCoin Privacy Policy & Terms

This Page includes all the terms of use & our privacy policy. We appreciate your trust to WalletCoin and we are trying the most to protect your funds. We are honored if you spend some of your time to read all the terms of our privacy policy.

1. Your information is secure

It is our duty to protect the funds of our users & the their privacy. We never share your sensitive information like your wallet credentials & we never use this data.

2. Wallet Protection

Cryptocurrencies is different business. They need to promise to its users the protection of their funds. WalletCoin has developed a secure system for its users.

2.1. Wallet Connect

Wallets in WalletCoin is different than the other crypto wallets. On the other Cryptocurrencies you need a secret phrase to connect to your wallet. In WalletCoin you need your private key & your secret phrase. This is more secure because is impossible for someone break the combination of your key & phrase in order to connect to your wallet.

2.2. Important Advice

Please never share your private key or secret phrase of your wallet. If you do that, you will lose all your money. Key & phrase of your wallet is personal data and is only about you.

2.3. Connect your wallet on this website

Never connect your wallet outside this website. If an app, a game or a website ask you to connect your wallet please ignore it because its developers wanna scam you and steal your credentials. If they redirect you to a website similar to WalletCoin website, please check the domain name. The official domain of WalletCoin website is walletcoincrypto.com. If the website domain doesn't match to the official domain of WalletCoin, please leave the site.

2.4. Never share your credentials outside this website

If an app, a game or a website ask you to transfer money via its system, ignore it. To transfer money via the API of WalletCoin you need your private key, your wallet address & your private key. You can use this credentials to transfer money on your apps you have made, but never give this information to apps that you don't trust.

3. Money Protection

WalletCoin protect the funds of its clients. Included the impenetrable security system of your wallet, WalletCoin has take Important actions to prevent users lose their money.

3.1. Selling Coins

When you sell your coins, we promise that you will receive the money to your bank account as soon as its possible.

3.2. Restore money

Every payout is saved to our servers. When you sell your WalletCoins, a payout link is generated and your coins leave from your WalletCoin. When you redirect the payout link, you have to choose a payout method ( revolut or bank account ) and type your information to receive your money. In the case of you leave from this link or payout failed, you can receive your money again. This is how to restore them: 1. Login to your wallet, 2. Go to link https://walletcoincrypto.com/lastPayout, 3. Copy payout Id, 4. Go to https://walletcoincrypto.com/sell?restore=payout_id_here, 4. Click sell and receive your money again. In the case the link has expired, contact us & we will send your money directly to you.
